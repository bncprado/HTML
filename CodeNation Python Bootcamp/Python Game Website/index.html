ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to Ogre's Quest: In Search of the Donkey, a thrilling adventure game where you, the player, embark on a journey through a dark and mysterious forest filled with hidden dangers and unexpected surprises. As you venture deeper into the forest, you will encounter a variety of characters, both friendly and foe alike. You will need to use your quick wit and cunning to navigate your way through this treacherous landscape, making decisions based on the clues you find and the random outcomes that occur. Every decision you make will have consequences, and with each new path you take, you will face new challenges and potential outcomes. The clock is ticking, and with only five minutes to find the donkey, you must act fast and make quick decisions if you hope to succeed. As you explore the forest, you will discover hidden paths, secret clues, and unexpected challenges. The forest is a mysterious place, and you never know what lies around the next corner. Will you encounter a friendly fairy who can help guide you on your way, or will you stumble upon a dangerous troll lurking in the shadows? The game is designed to be replayable, offering a different experience each time you play. With so many paths to explore and potential outcomes to uncover, the game is filled with endless possibilities and challenges. Ogre's Quest: In Search of Donkey is not for the faint of heart, but for those brave enough to face the challenges ahead, the rewards are well worth the risk. So, are you ready to embark on this exciting adventure and discover the secrets hidden within the forest? The clock is ticking, and time is running out. The fate of the donkey rests in your hands!


Pick between multiple directions and consider your next steps. Will you find the donkey on time? If you choose to travel North, you are given a directive to follow THE MUDDY FOOT PATH. The path is slippery and treacherous, but you push forward, hoping to find clues that will lead you to the donkey. As you make your way through the forest, you encounter Snakes, some friendly and some foes. They give you random clues that might lead you to the CAVES, or continue along THE MUDDY FOOT PATH towards the SWAMP. You take in the information and make quick decisions, hoping to find the donkey before time runs out. Before reaching the SWAMP, you find Blood on the ground, which could be a clue that leads you to the CAVE where the donkey is found, or to the SWAMP where you meet Ogre who tells you that it's a dead end due to flesh-eating fish. You weigh your options and decide to take your chances and go to the CAVE.


If you choose to travel South, you are given a directive to follow THE ROCKY FOOT PATH. You find Footprints, which could lead you to the SWAMP, the FARM, or continue along THE ROCKY FOOT PATH that leads to FIND THE DONKEY AND WIN THE GAME. Further along THE ROCKY FOOT PATH, you might meet Bates, who could be a friend or foe, and if you continue, you'll find the CAVES where the donkey is located.


Choosing to travel West, you are given a directive to follow THE DANGEROUS OBSTACLE PATH. This path is full of dangers, including Poisonous Spiders, who might attack and kill you or not attack at all. You might also encounter the Horse, who could be a friend or a foe. As you push forward, you find Blood on the ground, which could lead you to the CAVE where the donkey is found, or to the TAVERN in the KINGDOM. If you continue, you'll find the donkey's rug, which could lead you to the FARM or to meet the Farmer, who might give you more information.


Finally, choosing to travel East, you follow the GRASS & MEADOWS PATH. Here, you might meet Rabbit, who could tell you that the donkey is eating hay or tell you to continue along the path. You might also encounter Birds, who could tell you to look in the CAVES for the donkey or continue along the GRASS & MEADOWS PATH. Eventually, you reach the TAVERN in the KINGDOM, but if you want to find the donkey, you must return to the beginning and choose a different direction. With a time limit of 5 minutes and various paths and potential outcomes, Ogre's Quest: In Search of Donkey offers an exciting and replayable adventure through a mysterious forest filled with both friendly and dangerous creatures. As the player, you must make quick decisions based on the clues you find and the random outcomes that occur. Will you find the donkey and win the game, or will you be forced to start again and try a different path? The choice is yours!
